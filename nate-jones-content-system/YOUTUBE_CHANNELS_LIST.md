## YouTube RSS Feed Format

n8n will convert these YouTube channel URLs to RSS feeds automatically:
```
Channel URL: https://www.youtube.com/@username
RSS Feed: https://www.youtube.com/feeds/videos.xml?channel_id=CHANNEL_ID
```

The scraper handles this conversion automatically!
